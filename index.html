Privacy or comfort?

Thanks to the current situation, with the quarantines and the pandemic. Little by little we notice the vitality of technology in our lives. There are many aspects where we can no longer imagine our life without technology, not only for the entertainment and comforts, but also because we achieve that the information reaches many parts in very Little time.Progressively the way of communication was changed. making that people connect from far away, facilitating jobs, projects and interpersonal relationships. "Social media applications, video conferencing, and voicemails are playing pivotal roles in bridging communication gaps", Nowadays, thanks to the pandemic, there are many means of communication such as "google meet" "zoom",if we weren't had that apps, our lives would be worst in the pandemic, probable we wouldn't can take virtual classes or work. Globalization and great scientific research have allowed humanity achievements as great as, possessing huge networks capable of communicating information in a matter of milliseconds to the other side of the world or creating vaccines in a matter of months. However, these advances do not come alone, along with technological growth has come an immense loss in terms of privacy. Nowadays, social networks, video calls and messaging applications have become an important pillar for almost every area of ​​our life, "irrespective of locations, people can now connect with each other on a personal level" (M. Breitstein, 2018) If we were not able to use these technologies, we probably would not be able to have virtual classes or work, We have turned these tools into means to share every minimum aspect of our lives, we give permission to any application to access our data, our location, our camera etc. If we were responsible, we would not have any risk using these platforms, but we have given our privacy and our data in exchange for some comforts.
